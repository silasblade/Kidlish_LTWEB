Write: Perfect score

"Have you ever made a mistake?" or "Have you ever messed up?"

Write a short paragraph about a time when you made a mistake or experienced a failure. How did you feel when it happened? What did you learn from the experience? Did you try again or give up? Share your reflections on the situation and how you overcame it, if at all.
